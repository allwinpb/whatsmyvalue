Salary of a Housewife

Women in most parts of the world still have to take on the bulk of the domestic work (child care, cooking, cleaning etc..). In fact, this is true even in developed countries. This work is of course "unpaid". Here's a little something that calculates the real economic value of this work.

Simply hover over each of the tasks listed and select the number of hours spent on that task per week. The monthly salary will be updated automatically.
